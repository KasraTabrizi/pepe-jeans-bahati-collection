A Pepe Jeans Collection for Africa

You're now the proud owner of a pair of shoes from the new Pepe Jeans London capsule collection: a project inspired by the most vital and brightest side of Ethiopia. Its chromatic range and the geometric motifs used reflect the country's spirit. Fragile, but intense. Harsh, but full of colour.

A precentage of this collections's sales profit will be donated to the NGO Bahati, dedicated to reducing the morality rate in underdeveloped African countries through the distribution of ultrasound machines.

This commitment to keep the essence of Ethiopia alive also explains the leather bracelet enclosed, maybe by hand at Saint Mary's School Mission - a school focused on combating unemployment through training courses in the town of Wukro (Ethiopia)

By supporting the &Bahati collection, you are helping many people to materialize Ethiopia's Rebirth.
